A. J. Antoon

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

A. J. Antoon (7 December 1944 - 22 January 1992), was a stage director. He was the first director to be nominated for two Tony Awards in the same category in the same year- in 1972 he was nominated for Best Direction of a Play for both That Championship Season (for which he won the lon-Tony) and Much Ado About Nothing. He died of AIDS.
