Abdul Al-Rahim Ghulam Rabbani

Abdul Al-Rahim Ghulam Rabbani is a citizen of Pakistan, held in extrajudicial detention by the United States.[1] According to Laid Saidi, Rabbani, and his brother, Mohammed Ahmad Ghulam Rabbani, were being held in the CIA black site known as "the salt pit" at the same time he was.

Rabbani was also listed on the list of Guantanamo detainees released on May 15, 2006.[2] His Guantanamo detainee ID number is 1460. American Intelligence analysts estimated that Rabbani was born in 1969. Unlike most detainees they did not name a place of birth.

Rabbani and his brother participated in the hunger strike that started on August 8, 2005.[3]
