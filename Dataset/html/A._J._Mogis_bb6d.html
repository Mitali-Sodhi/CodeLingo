A. J. Mogis

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

A.J. Mogis is a musician from Omaha, Nebraska. He currently plays bass guitar in the indie-rock band Criteria. His brother is Mike Mogis.

They founded Presto! Recording Studios together and have recorded almost all the albums released by Saddle Creek Records as well as friends'. They were also members of Lullaby for the Working Class. Criteria has so far released two albums.
