Hazhir

Abdolhosein Hazhir (1899 â€“ November 5, 1949) was a Prime Minister of Iran.

He served as Minister 10 times, and eventually as Prime Minister.

When serving as Minister of Royal Court (darbar), he was assassinated at the age of 50 by Sayyed Hosein Emami Esfahani, who was a member of Navab Safavi's militant organization in 1949.
