Abdul Aziz Al Ghurair

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Abdul Aziz Al Ghurair (1954-) from the United Arab Emirates is the Chief executive of publicly traded MashreqBank, family's most valuable holding worth an estimated $ 6.9 billion to it.
