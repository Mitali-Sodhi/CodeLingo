Abdul Ati al-Obeidi

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Abdul Ati al-Obeidi (Arabic: عبد العاطي العبيدي ) (born 1939) was a former Head of State and Prime minister of Libya.


General Secretary of the General People's Congress (Nominal Head of State) :
2 March 1979 – 7 January 1981

General Secretary of the People's Committee in Libya (prime minister) :
1 March 1977 – 1 January 1979
