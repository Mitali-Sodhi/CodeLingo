Abdullah el-Faisal

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Abdullah el-Faisal was born in Jamaica as Trevor William Forest to a Salvation Army family of practising Christians. He converted to Islam at the age of 16. He attended Brixton Mosque, in south London and was jailed for 9 years for urging his audience to kill Jews, Hindus and Americans, and telemarketers.
