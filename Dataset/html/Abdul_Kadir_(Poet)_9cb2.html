Abdul Kadir (Poet)

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Abdul Kadir, (1906-1984) is a Bangladeshi poet.

[edit] Life

He graduated from the University of Dhaka. Was a teacher, officer of media department, editor of `Mahenou'.
