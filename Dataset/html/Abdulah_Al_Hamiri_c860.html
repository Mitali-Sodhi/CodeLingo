Abdulah Al Hamiri

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Abdulah Al Hamiri is a citizen of the United Arab Emirates held in extrajudicial detention in the United States Guantanamo Bay Naval Base, in Cuba.[1] His Guantanamo detainee ID number is 037. The Department of Defense reports that he was born on October 25, 1979, in Alan, United Arab Emirates.

Officials in Abu Dhabi confirm that Al Hamiri is a UAE citizen.[2]
