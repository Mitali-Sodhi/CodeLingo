A. J. Buckley

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

A.J. Buckley (February, 1978, Dublin) is Irish-American actor of television and film. Some of his TV appearances include The X-Files, NYPD Blue, Without a Trace and CSI:NY as Adam Ross. Film appearances include Disturbing Behavior, The Forsaken, Extreme Days and the Sci Fi Channel's Silent Warnings. Next he will lend his talents as a voice actor to the upcoming Happy Feet.

Buckley migrated from Ireland his with family at age six. He now lives in Los Angeles.
