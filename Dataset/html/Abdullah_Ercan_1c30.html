Abdullah Ercan (born 8 December 1971 in Istanbul) is a Turkish football player.

He played mostly for Trabzonspor and Fenerbahçe.

He played for Turkey national football team and was a participant at Euro 1996, Euro 2000 and 2002 FIFA World Cup.
