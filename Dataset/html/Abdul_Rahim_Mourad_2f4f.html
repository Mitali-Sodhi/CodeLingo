Abdul Rahim Mourad

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Abdul Rahim Mourad (Arabic: عبد الرحيم مراد; from the Beqaa Valley) is a former Defense Minister to Lebanon, and he was also a former minister of Education and Higher Studies. He is also the founder of Lebanese International University.
