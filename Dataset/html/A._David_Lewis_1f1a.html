A. David Lewis

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

A. David Lewis (born 1977 in Boston, MA) is an American comic book & graphic novel writer and educator.

Raised in Framingham, MA, Lewis is the founder of the Caption Box comic book imprint. In addition, Lewis has served as both an instructor at such institutions as Georgetown University and Northeastern University as well as a lecturer at academic conferences across the country. He is an Editorial Board member for the International Journal of Comic Art under Editor John Lent. In Fall 2006, he started a PhD program in Religion & Literature at Boston University.

[edit] Awards

  • 2003 Cinescape Literary Genre Competition
  • 2004 Broken Frontier Award (nee Paper Screen Gems)
  • 2005 Howard E. Day Prize
  • 2006 Gay Comic Writers Guild Man of the Year.
