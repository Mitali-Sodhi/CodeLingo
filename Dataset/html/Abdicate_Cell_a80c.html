Abdicate Cell

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Abdicate Cell is a netlabel, a music publishing experiment mainly dedicated to experimental or extreme music (difficult listening, abstract, sonic landscapes, grindcore, electro-crust, etc.).

Formed by Sergi Saldaña Massó (aka Zherji) it began in Barcelona in 1998 as a way to record "little sonic experiences". The principle is to experiment, learn, search, collaborate, express, (re)place sounds in time. Abdicate Cell has been based in Norway since 2004.

In 2005, it was consolidated as a netlabel. Most of Abdicate Cell's recordings are under Creative Commons Music Sharing License. All the avaible recordings are hosted at archive.org.
