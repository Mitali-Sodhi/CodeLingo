A'isha (name)

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

A'isha (Arabic: عائشة‎ ​) is an Arabic female given name that means "she who lives". Other tranliterations include Aisha, Ayşe, Ayesha, 'A'isha, 'Aisha, `ā'isha or Aishah'.

People named A'isha include:

  • Aisha, a wife of the Islamic prophet Muhammad
  • A'isha bint Talhah
  • Aisha Kahlil, a singer
  • Aisha Tyler, the American comedian and actress
