A. Gary Anderson Graduate School of Management

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

The A. Gary Anderson Graduate School of Management is located at the University of California, Riverside. The origin of AGSM can be traced back to 1970 when UC Riverside established the Graduate School of Administration. In 1994, The A. Gary Anderson Foundation gave a generous endowment to the school and it was named after the founder of Director's Mortgage.

Today the school offers an innovative MBA program and an interdisciplinary undergraduate major in Business Administration in conjunction with the College of Humanities, Arts & Social Sciences.

AGSM is the home of the UCR Sloan Center for Internet Retailing and eLab 2.0, a pioneering online laboratory for consumer behavior research.

The A. Gary Anderson Graduate School of Management provides:

Personal interaction between faculty and graduate students; Individual attention to students; One of the best equipped business school environments in the country; Four UC Riverside Libraries with more than 2 million bound volumes and one of the best electronic research facilities in the nation; Career planning and placement as well as career development and internship placement.
