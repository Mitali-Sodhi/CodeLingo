Abdeslam Ouaddou, born November 1st, 1978 in Ksar Askour, Morocco is a Moroccan football player currently playing for Greek Super League side Olympiacos. His usual position is defender.

He previously had a spell in the English Premiership with Fulham FC. During his career he has also played for French Ligue 1 clubs AS Nancy and Stade Rennais.

He started his footballing career quite late, at the age of 21.
