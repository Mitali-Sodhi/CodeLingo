Abdoulaye Soulama

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Abdoulaye Soulama (born November 29, 1974) is a Burkinan football player who, as of 2004, was playing for ASFB.

He was a member of the Burkinan 2004 African Nations Cup team, who finished bottom of their group in the first round of competition, thus failing to secure qualification for the quarter-finals. At 2001-2002 football season, Abdoulaye played for Turkish side Denizlispor. He played successful matches at the club which made it UEFA Cup that season. Unfortunately he had some rows with other players. Some Turkish players, including team captain Tolunay Kafkas, wanted coach Sakip Ozberk to give the uniform to Turkish substitute Suleyman Kucuk. At cup match again Fenerbahce SK, Tolunay punched Abdoulaye in the face and got red card. After reducing 10 man, Denizlispor managed beat their strong opponent 2-1. At the end of the season Abdoulaye left the club.
