Abdullah S. Jum'ah

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Abdullah S. Jum'ah is the current President, Director, and CEO of Saudi Aramco, the world's largest oil company. In 1995, he succeeded Ali Al-Naimi, the first Saudi to ever become the president of the Saudi newly government acquired, Aramco. Abdallah S. Jum'ah is the president and chief executive officer of Saudi Aramco. He joined the company in 1968 and was appointed president in November 1995. Mr. Jum'ah is a member of the Saudi Aramco Board of Directors. He is a graduate of American University in Beirut and completed the Program for Management Development at Harvard University in 1976.
