Abderrahmane Hammad

Bronze 2000 Sydney High Jump

Abderrahmane Hammad (Arabic: عبدالرحمن حمد‎) (born May 27, 1977 in Dellys) is an Algerian athlete competing in the high jump.

[edit] Achievements

  • 2004 Pan Arab Games - gold medal
  • 2002 IAAF Grand Prix Final - third place
  • 2002 African Championships - gold medal
  • 2000 Summer Olympics - bronze medal
  • 2000 African Championships - gold medal
  • 1999 All-Africa Games - silver medal
  • 1998 African Championships - gold medal
