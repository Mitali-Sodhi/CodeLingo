Abdul Rahim al-Sharqawi

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Abdul Rahim al-Sharqawi is an alleged member of the al Qaeda leadership.[1] According to MSNBC al-Sharqawi.is one of the 25 top al Qaeda leadership members. According to MSNBC al-Sharqawi.is called "Riyadh the Facilitator," According to MSNBC al-Sharqawi.is said to have been captured before April 2002.
