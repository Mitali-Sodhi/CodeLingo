Abdul Latif Dayfallah

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Abdul Latif Dayfallah (Arabic: عبد اللطيف ضيف الله) (b. 1930) was the Prime Minister of the Yemen Arab Republic for 26 April to 5 October 1963, under President Abdullah as-Sallal.

Dayfallah also served as acting Prime Minister for nine days in January of 1975.
