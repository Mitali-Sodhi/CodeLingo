Abdulaziz Al-Janoubi (Arabic: عبدالعزيز الجنوبي‎) (born October 25, 1976) was a Saudi Arabian footballer in defense role.

He was played for Al-Nasr club and, for national team he participated at 1998 FIFA World Cup.
