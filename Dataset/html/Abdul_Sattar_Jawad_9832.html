Abdul Sattar Jawad is a research professor in the John Hope Franklin Center for Interdisciplinary and International Studies at Duke University. He teaches Arabic literature and is an expert on the works of T.S. Eliot and William Shakespeare, and on Iraqi media and academia. Before coming to Duke, he was a professor at Mustansiriya University in Baghdad and edited the Baghdad Mirror.
