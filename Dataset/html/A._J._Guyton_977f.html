A. J. Guyton

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Arthur James (A.J.) Guyton (born February 13, 1978 in Peoria, Illinois) is a professional basketball player who was selected by the Chicago Bulls in the 2nd round (32nd overall pick) in the 2000 NBA Draft. He played collegiately at Indiana University and was named Big Ten Co-MVP during his senior year, averaging 19.7 ppg. Guyton played for the Bulls from 2000-2002 and briefly for the Golden State Warriors during the 2002-03 season.
