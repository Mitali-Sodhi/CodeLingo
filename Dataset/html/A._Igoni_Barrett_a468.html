A. Igoni Barrett

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Andre Igoni Barrett (born March 26, 1979) is a Nigerian writer. He was born to a Nigerian mother and a Jamaican father in the coastal city of Port Harcourt, in Nigeria's Niger delta. His short fiction has been widely published on the web. He was a winner of the BBC World Service Short Story competition for 2005.

His first book, a collection of short stories titled From Caves Of Rotten Teeth, was published in 2005. He lives in Nigeria.
