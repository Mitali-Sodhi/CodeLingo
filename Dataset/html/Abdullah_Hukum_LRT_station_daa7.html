Abdullah Hukum LRT station

Abdullah Hukum is an LRT station in Kuala Lumpur, Malaysia that is served by Rapid KL's Kelana Jaya Line.

There is a proposal to construct a pedestrian bridge from this station to the Mid Valley Megamall. A KTM Komuter station is also expected to be constructed here.[1]
