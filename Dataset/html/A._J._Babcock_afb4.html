A. J. Babcock

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

A.J. Babcock is the former bassist for the Christian rock band House of Heroes. He started the band in 2002 with Tim Skipper and Colin Rigsby. Before that time, the band had been known as No Tagbacks. Babcock was also the band's primary lyricist. In 2006, he wanted to stop touring, and was replaced by Colin Rigsby's brother, Jared Rigsby. Babcock has now left the band, as of October 2006. He now has a new band named Flowerdagger.
