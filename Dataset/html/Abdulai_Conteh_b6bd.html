Abdulai Conteh

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Dr Abdulai Osman Conteh is a lawyer and politician from Sierra Leone.

His roles in Sierra Leone politics include Minister of Foreign Affairs; Minister of Finance, and Attorney-General and Minister of Justice.

In 2000 he became Chief Justice of the Supreme Court of Belize.
