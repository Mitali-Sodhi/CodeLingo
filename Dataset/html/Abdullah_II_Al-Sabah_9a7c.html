Sheikh Abdullah II bin Sabah Al-Sabah (1814-May 1892) was the fifth emir of Kuwait and eldest son of Sabah II Al-Sabah who he succeeded. He ruled from November, 1866 to May, 1892. He accepted Ottoman suzerainty in 1871 through which he received the title of Pasha.
