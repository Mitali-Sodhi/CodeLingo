Abdul Rahman Saleh

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

For the national hero of the same name, see Abdul Rahman Saleh (hero).

Abdul Rahman Saleh (born 1 April 1941 in Pekalongan) is the current Attorney General of Indonesia for the period 2004-2009. He graduated from Gadjah Mada University.
