Abdul Momin

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Abdul Momin (1852 – May 30, 1885) was the sultan of Brunei from 1852 until his death. He was the son of a nobleman named Pengiran Shahbandar. Abdul Mahman succeeded his cousin Omar Ali Saifuddin II as sultan upon the latter's death, having previously served as regent for him during his ill health.
