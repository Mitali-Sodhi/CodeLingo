Abderrahman Sadik Karim

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Abderrahman Sadik Karim was Minister of Environment in the first cabinet appointed by the Interim Iraq Governing Council in September 2003. A Kurd, Karim is an engineer and environmental activist.
