Abdul Majid Muhammad

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

It has been suggested that this article or section be merged with Abdul Majid Muhammed. (Discuss)

Abdul Majid Muhammad is an Iranian held in extrajudicial detention in the United States Guantanamo Bay Naval Base, in Cuba.[1]

[edit] Combatant Status Review Tribunal

Muhammad chose to participate in his Combatant Status Review Tribunal.

Muhammad was accused of serving as a night watchman for the Taliban.[1]

Muhammad said he had been a drug-dealer in Iran, who had traveled to Afghanistan to buy drugs.
