Abdukadyr Orozbekov

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Abdukadyr Orozbekov (1889-1938) was the first president of Kyrgyz SSR (1927 - 1937). He was born (1889) in Ohna (ОХНА Айылы) village , Kadamjai district.
