Abdoulaye Djire

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Abdoulaye Djire nickname Junior (b. 28 February 1981 in Abidjan) is a Côte d'Ivoire football (soccer) player who as of 2006 plays for FC Metalurh Donetsk. He has been playing for the Côte d'Ivoire national football team since 2002.

[edit] Clubs

  • 1999-2003: ASEC Mimosas
  • 2003-2006: K.S.K. Beveren
  • 2006-present: FC Metalurh Donetsk
