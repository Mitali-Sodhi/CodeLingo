Abdulfez

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Abdulfez, aka Cliff Taylor, is best known as having been the host of MTV Europe in London when it launched in 1987 all over Europe as the first ever music show to beam down from outer space. His band the Fez Brothers in 1986 hosted another "voice" under the guise of Max Headroom aka Matt Frewer.

Abdulfez also did shows on BBC Radio 4 hosted by Ned Sharin, and as a weekly guest on variety show the Funky Bunker hosted by Craig Charles on ITV-1 network in London. Central Television's Spitting Image followed with an Emmy Nomination for best International Comedy for "Bumbledown: The lives and Times of Ronald Reagan.
