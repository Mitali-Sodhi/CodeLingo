A. J. John Memorial High School (A.J.J.M.H.S) is a high school at Chattengottunada in Kavilumpara village, Kozhikode district. It is managed by a Christian organization, the Missionary Congregation of the Blessed Sacrament (or MCBS Sabha). It is an aided school, and hence run by government funding. Government, however, has no role in management of this school (in Kerala there is a system where the government funds schools, but all expenditures go to private and religious management).

Many students from wealthy families attend this school. The school has demonstrated solid performance in academic as well as extra-curricular activities. The school is on the banks of a beautiful river in a location teeming with nature.
