Abdullah Sadiq

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Dr Sadiq at Intel Malaysia Summer of 2006

Dr. Abdullah Sadiq is a leading Pakistani Physicist and ICTP laureate who received the ICTP Prize in honour of Nikolaj N. Bogolubov, in the fields of Mathematics and Solid State Physics in 1987 for his contributions to scientific knowledge in the field of Solid State Physics. He did his early education from Islamia Collegiate School, Islamia College Peshawar and the Physics Department at the University of Peshawar and got his PhD in 1971 from the University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign, USA. Dr. Sadiq is currently the Rector of Ghulam Ishaq Khan Institute of Engineering Sciences and Technology in Topi, Pakistan.
