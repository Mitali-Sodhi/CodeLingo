A-Treat Bottling Company

The A-Treat Bottling Company is a relatively small producer of various soft drinks, headquartered in Allentown, Pennsylvania. Founded in 1918 by Joseph Egizio, the company distinguishes itself by producing sodas in the traditional manner, for example using essential oils and cane sugar, rather than extracts and high fructose corn syrup. A-Treat produces soft drinks in at least 16 different flavors: Blue, Birch Beer, Black Cherry, Champagne Kola, Cola, Cream soda, Fruit Punch, Ginger Ale, Grape, Grapefruit, White Birch Beer, Pineapple, Sarsparilla, Orange, Treat-up, and Root Beer. Most, if not all, of these flavors are also produced in a diet formulation. [1]

A-Treat beverages have spotty distribution throughout Pennsylvania and New Jersey.

A-Treat Birch Beer and Sarsaparilla are rated highly by connoisseurs of these types of beverages. [2]
