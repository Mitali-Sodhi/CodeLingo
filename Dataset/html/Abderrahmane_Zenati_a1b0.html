Abderrahmane Zenati

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Abderrahmane Zenati is a painter and writer from Morocco, born in Oujda in 1943.

Zenati was born in a poor family without a father. He did not learn to read and write until he was twelve. He worked as an assistant nurse at an hospital in Oudja. He never followed a professional training as writer or painter.
