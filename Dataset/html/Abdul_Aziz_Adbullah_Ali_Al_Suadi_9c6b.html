Abdul Aziz Adbullah Ali Al Suadi

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Abdul Aziz Adbullah Ali Al Suadi is a Yemeni electrician detained in the American naval base at Guantanamo Bay, Cuba.[1]

No formal charges have been laid against Al Suadi, but his Combatant Status Review Tribunal contains the following allegations:

  1. The detainee is an electrician from Yemen who traveled from his home to Afghanistan via Syria and Iran in September 2000.
  2. The detainee reported to the al-Farouq training facility upon his arrival in Afghanistan.
  3. The detainee participated in advanced training at the al-Qaida Tarnak Farms training facility in 2001

The notes from the officer assigned to learn Al Suadi's side of events said that, initially, he was going to be present for his Combatant Status Review Tribunal, but that he changed his mind. The extremely brief handwritten notes he submitted to the tribunal characterized the interview as "circuitous".
