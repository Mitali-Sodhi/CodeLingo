Abdullah Kurshumi

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Abdullah Kurshumi (Arabic: عبد الله كرشمي) was the Prime Minister of the Yemen Arab Republic from 2 September 1969 until 5 February 1970. He served under President Abdul Rahman al-Iryani.
