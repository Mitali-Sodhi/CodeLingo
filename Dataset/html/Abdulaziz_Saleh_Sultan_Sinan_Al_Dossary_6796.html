Abdulaziz Saleh Sultan Sinan Al Dossary

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Lieutenant Colonel Abdulaziz Saleh Sultan Sinan Al Dossary was an F16 fighter pilot in the Bahrain Defence Force whose plane disappeared on September 27, 2003.[1]

Al Dossary's name rose to notability in May 2006 when rumors surfaced that he was alive and being held in clandestine extrajudicial detention in one of the United States black sites.

Both the Bahrain Defense Force and US spokesmen have denied the rumors. An extensive search for Al Dossary's plane had been carried out. Al Dossary had no known history of extremism.
