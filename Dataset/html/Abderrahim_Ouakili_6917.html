Abderrahim Ouakili

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Abderrahim Ouakili (Arabic: عبد الرحيم الوكيلي) (born December 11, 1970) is a Moroccan football player, who played for several teams, including FSV Mainz, 1860 München, TeBe Berlin and Karlsruher SC, all in Germany.

He played for Morocco national football team and was a participant at the 1998 FIFA World Cup.
