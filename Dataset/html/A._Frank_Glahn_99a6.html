A. Frank Glahn

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

A. Frank Glahn(born 1865 - 1941), was a German mystisist, Germanic revivalist and most notably a Pendulum dowser. He was used by the German military in the Third Reich, not nessesarily willingly.

Glahn was mentioned greatly in the book Reveal the Power of the Pendulum by Karl Spiesberger.

In 1920 A. Frank-Glahn's "Das Deutsche Tarot Buch" was published by Uranus Verlag, accompanied by a deck of cards "Deutsches Original Tarot", an Egyptain style deck, but unique. Glahn's book and cards became a German tarot bible, which survived up in the 1980's, published by Hermann Bauer Verlag. (K.Frank Jensen)
