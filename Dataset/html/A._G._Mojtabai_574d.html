A. G. Mojtabai

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Ann Grace Mojtabai (b. 1937) is an American Jewish novelist, author of Blessed Assurance, which concerns nuclear weapons manufacture and fundamentalist religious belief, and the forthcoming novel Ordinary Time.[1] In addition, Mojtabai taught for many years at the University of Tulsa and has written articles on writing in academic journals.
