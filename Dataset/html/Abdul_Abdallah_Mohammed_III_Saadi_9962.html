Abdul Abdallah Mohammed III Saadi

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Abdul Abdallah Mohammed III or Abou Fares Abdellah (Arabic: عبد العبدالله محمد سعدي الثالث) was Sultan of Morocco from 1603 to 1608, and was a member of the Saadi Dynasty. He was from the Saguia el-Hamra region in Western Sahara.
