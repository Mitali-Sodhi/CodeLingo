Abdul Rehman (born January 2, 1987 in Ajman) is a United Arab Emirates cricketer.

A wicketkeeper and useful lower-order right-handed batsman, Rehman was plucked from the obscurity of the United Arab Emirates Under 17s squad into the national team for the 2004 Asia Cup.

He made his One-day International debut for the UAE against a strong Indian team but wasn't selected in the team for the next game against tournament hosts Sri Lanka.
