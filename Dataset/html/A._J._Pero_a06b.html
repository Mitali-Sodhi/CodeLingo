A. J. Pero

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Anthony Jude Pero (born October 14, 1959) is the drummer of the American heavy metal band Twisted Sister. He joined the band in 1981, after he left Cities, a local New York City band. He left the band in 1986 and re-joined Cities. He played with Dee Snider's SMFs and reunited with Twisted Sister in 2002.
