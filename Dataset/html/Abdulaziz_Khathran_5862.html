Abdulaziz Khathran (born 31 July 1973) (Arabic: عبد العزيز الخثران) is a Saudi Arabian football (soccer) player who currently plays as a defender for Al Hilal in the Saudi league.

Khathran began his career at Al Shabab where he started out as a left-back, before being nurtured into a left midfield role. For his national team, he usually plays as a defensive midfielder.

He was recently called up to the Saudi Arabian team for the 2006 FIFA World Cup.
