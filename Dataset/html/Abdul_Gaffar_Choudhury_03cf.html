Abdul Gaffar Choudhury

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Abdul Gaffar Choudhury(1934), is a renowned Bangladeshi author, columnist, author of Amar Bhaier Rokte Rangano, the main song of commemorating the Language Movement. He lives in London and writes many column including 'Tritio Mot'.
