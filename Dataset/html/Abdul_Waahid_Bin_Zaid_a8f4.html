Abdul Waahid Bin Zaid

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Abdul Waahid Bin Zaid Abul Fadl (d. 793) was an early day Sufi Saint, a successor to Hasan al-Basri, second link in the Sufi Silsilah of Chishti Order, and the Master of Fudhail Bin Iyadh.

He is also reported to have received education from Imam Abu Hanifah.

His year of birth is unknown but is said to be on 176, 177, or 186 Hijri. The month is said to be 27th of Safar. His shrine is in Basrah, Iraq.
