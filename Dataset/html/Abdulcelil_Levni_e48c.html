Abdulcelil Levni

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Abdulcelil Celebi (died 1732), also known as Levni, was an Ottoman painter and miniaturist. He was court painter in the Topkapi Palace under Sultans Mustafa II and Ahmed III.
