Abdoul Salam Sow

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Abdoul Salam Sow (born August 13, 1970) is a Guinean football player who, as of 2004, was not affiliated with a football club.

He was part of the Guinean 2004 African Nations Cup team, who finished second in their group in the first round of competition, before losing in the quarter finals to Mali.

Sow's previous clubs include Ankaragucu, Martigues, Belenenses, Imortal and Qatar SC
