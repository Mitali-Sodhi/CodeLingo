A. H. Bulbulian Residence.

The A. H. Bulbulian Residence is a house in Rochester, Minnesota designed by Frank Lloyd Wright. It is down the street from another Wright design (the Thomas Keys house) and is an example of Wright's Usonian genre of architecture. The house presently is painted pink, a color not likely specified by Wright.
