Abdullah Ahmed Abdullah

Abdullah Ahmed Abdullah (Arabic: عبدالله أحمد عبدالله; born about 1963) is an Egyptian national wanted by the United States government.

He is also known as Abu Mohamed Al-Masri, Saleh, and Abu Mariam.

He is wanted by the United States government in connection to the August 7, 1998 American embassy bombings in Dar es Salaam Tanzania and Nairobi Kenya. He had fled Nairobi on August 6, 1998 for Karachi, Pakistan and has since disappeared. He is suspected of being in Afghanistan.

On October 10, 2001, Abdullah was placed on the initial list of the FBI's top 22 Most Wanted Terrorists, which was released to the public by President George W. Bush.
