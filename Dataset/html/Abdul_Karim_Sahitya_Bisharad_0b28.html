[edit] Life

Because of poverty, Abdul Karim Sahitya Bisharad could not continue his education. He became a simple employee of a court and school inspectors office.

[edit] Literary career

Abdul Karim was a collector of ancient Bengali Literature. He collected more then 2,000 Puthis to prevent them from being lost.

More then 1,000 of them were written by Bengali Muslims. No other person or organization had collected this number of Puthis before.

His literary works are included in the curriculum of school level, secondary, higher secondary, and graduation level Bengali Literature in Bangladesh.

[edit] References
