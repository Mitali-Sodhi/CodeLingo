Abdikokhor Marifaliev

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Abdikokhor Marifaliev (born 1 May 1974) is an Uzbekistani football defender.

He got 32 caps and 5 goals for the national team between 1992 and 2001 [1].

[edit] Clubs

  • Dustlik Toshkent
  • FC Yesil Bogatyr Petropavlovsk
  • Lokomotiv Toshkent
