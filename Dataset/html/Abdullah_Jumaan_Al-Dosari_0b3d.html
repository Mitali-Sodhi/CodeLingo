Abdullah Jumaan Al-Dosari

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Abdullah Jumaan Al-Dosari (Arabic: عبد الله جمعان الدوسري) (born 10 November 1977) is a Saudi Arabian football player.

He played most of his career for Al Hilal.

He played for the Saudi Arabia national football team and was a participant at the 2002 FIFA World Cup.
