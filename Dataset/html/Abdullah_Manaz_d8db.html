Abdullah Manaz (born 1958 in Turkey) is a philosophy doctor who graduated from Divinity Faculty of Ankara University, in 1984. Manaz has been working as in Turkish radio and television for 20 years. He has written 8 books about various topics, prepared television programs and given lectures around the world. His Doctorate Thesis is about Role of Wisdom about Understanding Qur'an.

[edit] Works

  • Thought Movements in Islam in Last Period, Turkish, Ankara (1989)
  • Turkish Historical Buildings in Damascus, Syria, Turkish and Arabic, Ankara (1992)
  • Ataturk Reforms and Islam, Turkish, Izmir (1995, 2006)
  • Political Islam in Turkey and the World, Turkish, Ankara (1998, 2005)
  • History of Political Zionism, Turkish, Istanbul (2006)
  • Strategical Map Collection, Turkish, Istanbul (2006)
