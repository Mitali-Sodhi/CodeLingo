A-Story, Bee-Story

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

A-Story, Bee-Story is the fifth episode of season six of the television situation comedy Will & Grace.

Spoiler warning: Plot and/or ending details follow.

[edit] Plot

Grace is sick with food poisoning and has to stay in bed. Jack enters a spelling contest and asks Karen to be his mentor.

[edit] Additional cast

Judge – Alex Boling
Guy – Bruce Dworkin
Contestant #1 – Mark Cirillo
Contestant #2 – Jase Wirey

[edit] Trivia

  • Debra Messing's (Grace) two scenes were actually filmed in her house as she was sick due to severe morning sickness
