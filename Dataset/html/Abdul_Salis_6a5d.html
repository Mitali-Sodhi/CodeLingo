Abdul Salis

Abdul Salis is a British actor. He has appeared in numerous roles in theatre, television and film, for example in Casualty, Trevor's World of Sport, Love Actually, Sahara and Doctor Who. He also appeared in the 2006 production of The Exonerated in the Riverside Studios in London.
