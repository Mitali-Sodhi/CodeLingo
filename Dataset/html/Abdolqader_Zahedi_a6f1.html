Abdolqader Zahedi

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

Abdolqader Zahedi (Born in Iran) was a high-ranking Sunni religious figure and Iranian Kurdish politician.

Abdolqader Zahedi was a member of Iran's Assembly of Experts.
